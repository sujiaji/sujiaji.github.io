My Chinese name is 苏佳骥. I am currently serving as a research fellow in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at the National University of Singapore (NUS). I have been to NUS since August 2018, and received my Ph.D. in Statistics here in Jan 2024, under the guidance of associate professor Zhigang YAO. Prior to my journey in Singapore, I completed my B.S. in Statistics from Zhejiang University in 2018. For additional details about my academic and professional journey, please refer to my CV.

My main interest is in statistical inference with singularity, including but not limited to manifold fitting in Euclidean spaces, real data dimension reduction, and ill-posed inverse problems. Apart from that, I am also interested in machine learning, especially neural networks, from both theory and application aspects.
